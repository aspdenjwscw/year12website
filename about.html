Website Title

Information about the website, and the creator!

Our purpose

This website was designed for WSC high-school students, giving them a free tool to manage, track and view all their assignments easily. The google classroom you use everyday is good, but not sufficient. It does not provide enough necessary tools for students to easily know what exactly they have to and when. Document

The tools

This website provides students with two primary tools, a to-do list / task tracking tool, and a calender. The to-do list is more detailed than the average to-do list. It has been designed for students that have assessments, so it will allow you to set a final due date, sub-dates for check-in points and details for each little part of your assessment. The calender is simple, but provides an overview of your assignments final due dates. There is in-depth customization.

About the creator

I am James, as of the time the website was created I was a year 12. I created this website as I know it will serve lots of people well, including me, which is one of the primary reasons I created it in the first place. I saw a need for a more sufficient system, so I sought out to create one.

Benefits it provides.

This website, with it's tools is designed to reduce stress and improve time management in students. With these, not only will your stress levels go down, promoting happiness but your grades will go up. This will make your parents happy.
