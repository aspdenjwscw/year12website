Our purpose

This website was designed to give students information about how to manage and track their assessments efficiently and effectively. It will give key and necessary information to ensure all students can ensure they are minimising stress and maximising time management. Having good time management and limited stress is extremely benefitial for mental and phsyical health, and practicing those is encouraged for all. Document

The tools

This website has a page where you can find a lot of information on how to reduce stress and manage your time effectively. This can be found on the home page. The website also includes a quiz with 20 questions based on the information given to you in the home page. Make sure to give the quiz a go!
